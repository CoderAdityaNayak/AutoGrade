Privacy Policy – AutoGrade v1.2

At AutoGrade, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data while using our service.

Data Processing: AutoGrade uses a third-party AI service (Groq AI) to process images you upload. Images are sent to Groq for processing only and are not stored there permanently by AutoGrade.

Temporary Storage: We use Firebase Storage to temporarily store images. These images are deleted immediately after processing, ensuring minimal retention.

No Personal Information Collected: In version 1.2, AutoGrade does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any other personal information.

Future Updates: In future versions, we may ask for personal information to enable new features. AutoGrade may introduce payment options due to the 75-image processing limit. In such cases, relevant information may be collected and securely stored to facilitate transactions.

Security: We take reasonable measures to keep your data safe while in transit and during temporary storage.

Your Rights: You can stop using AutoGrade at any time. In future versions where personal data is collected, you will have the right to access, modify, or delete your information.
